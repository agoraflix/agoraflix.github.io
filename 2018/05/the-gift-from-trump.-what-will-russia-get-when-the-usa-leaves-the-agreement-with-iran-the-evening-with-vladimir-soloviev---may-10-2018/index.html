The gift from Trump. What will Russia get when the USA leaves the agreement with Iran? The evening with Vladimir Soloviev - May 10, 2018

May 10, 2018 by Team Agoraflix
Tags: Vladimir Soloviev middle east nuclear deal iran usa
Categories: russia

Vladimir Soloviev and his guests are discussing the current situation in the Middle East, including the problem of the nuclear deal with Iran, expansion of the USA, and other important problems of the region. They are trying to predict what’s going to happen, and whether the USA will attack Iran, potentially causing a new catastrophe in the region.

Vladimir Soloviev

One of the most popular TV hosts in Russia, who worked for several radio stations and TV channels, and about 6 years ago was invited to the State Company VGTRK (Russia 1, Russia 2, Vesti.FM and other projects). In the past, he expressed different opinions about the regime, sometimes supporting it, and sometimes fighting it.

Said Gafurov

A russian economist, sociologist, orientalist, politician, bureaucrat and opera critic. Mostly he supports the USA, and critics the current regime.

Elena Suponina

An expert in Eastern cultures and countries. She’s a specialist in Islam. Currently she holds the position of a deputy head of the Russian Institute for Strategic Studies.

Igor Morozov

A russian politician, member of the Federation Council of the Ryazan Oblast (2012—2017), candidate for governor of the Ryazan region (in 2004 and 2012). Expert in the field of international economic relations, the author of several scientific papers.

Sergey Mikheev

An expert in the field of political science. He’s a frequent guest of various TV shows and programs. He support the current government and prefers to keep a loyal position.

Sargon Hadaya

Russia Today reporter. Works in Syria and other countries of the Middle East. His main employer is RT Arabic.

Yakov Kedmi

A specialist in political science. He used to work in Special Forces in Israel. He is considered an under cover KGB agent.

Dmitry Evstafiev

A specialist in political science. He’s on the faculty of the National Research University Higher School of Economics.
