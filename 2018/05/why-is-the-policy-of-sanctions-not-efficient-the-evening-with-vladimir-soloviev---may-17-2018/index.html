Why is the policy of sanctions not efficient? The evening with Vladimir Soloviev - May 17, 2018

May 17, 2018 by Team Agoraflix
Tags: Vladimir Soloviev russia sanctions central asia
Categories: russia

Guests of the program discuss the current models of the USA and Russia development, trying to find the best one. They want to find out how to cooperate with the countries of Central Asia, and avoid new conflicts in this region.

Vladimir Soloviev

One of the most popular TV hosts in Russia, who worked for several radio stations and TV channels, and about 6 years ago was invited to the State Company VGTRK (Russia 1, Russia 2, Vesti.FM and other projects). In the past, he expressed different opinions about the regime, sometimes supporting it, and sometimes fighting it.

Andrey Sidorov

Active supporter of the government, he is an expert on international politics at Moscow State University and one of several hawkish Russian analysts.

Mikhail Remizov

The president of the Institute of National Strategy in Moscow. Never had a clear position, prefers to adjust his opinion to the current situation.

Nikolay Zlobin

He currently serves as founder and president of the Center on Global Interests in Washington, D.C. He is the author of more than a dozen books and more than 300 essays and articles on topics of 20th century history, Russian and American politics, and international security.

Igor Korotchenko

Editor in chief of the National Defense magazine. Active supporter of the government, and (supposedly) an active officer of Special Services in Russia.

Alexander Sagomonyan

Historian, political scientist, Professor of the Institute of International Relations and Social-Political Studies, Moscow State Linguistics University.

Sergey Mikheev

An expert in the field of political science. He’s a frequent guest of various TV shows and programs. He support the current government and prefers to keep a loyal position.
